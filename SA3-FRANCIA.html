TIPS FOR OUR USERS


Make Friends!

PGR's playerbase isn't too huge, but that won't be an issue considering how friendly and willing to help our community is. Don't be afraid to ask questions on our forum and Discord server if ever you have any queries and questions.

Jam Out!

PGR has some great music! Always keep that volume high, and pay close attention to have the time of your life! While you're at it, subscribe to Vanguard Sound Studio's YouTube channel to stay updated about any new OSTs added to the game!

Have Fun!

Most importantly, while playing PGR, always remember to have fun, either by yourself, or with your friends! Be sure to take breaks when you get tired, and don't be afraid to take a step back to breathe.
